Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

An easy, low-cost spaghetti and meatball recipe with a simple tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp dried mix herbs

Steps

  1. Heat a large saucepan over a medium heat and add the olive oil.
  2. Mix together the beef, garlic and herbs.
  3. Heat a frying pan over a medium heat and add the olive oil.
